ENVIROMENT AND YOUNG CUBANS




Cuba intrigued us because we sensed that young Cubans were… a lot of things. So we created a little word game about it. It all started with a lot of serendipity…We went to Cuba to learn about young people’s attitudes towards climate change and what they wanted to do about it.



Chapter 1: Trip

In our first exploratory visit, we came face to face with incredible ‘youth leaders’ in Cuba’s environmental sector (they come in all shapes and sizes!). The problem is they are dispersed all over the country so working together is hard. It doesn't help that communication and transportation kind of suck.
